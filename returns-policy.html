Returns Policy

You will have 7 days from the time your order is delivered, to return your item(s). You must ensure your item(s) are returned in same condition that they were sent out. Item(s) must be sealed within the plastic packaging and envelope they came in. Failure to do so may result in your return being rejected and no refund being issued.

Please note, you will ONLY be refunded for the item(s) purchased. ALL delivery charges are non-refundable.

If you have any queries about delivery or returns, please feel free to message us and we will be happy to help.
